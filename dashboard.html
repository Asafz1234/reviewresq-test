How to use ReviewResQ daily
Simple steps your staff can follow after each job or visit.
  1. Open your ReviewResQ link on your phone or tablet (or print the QR code).
  2. After each job, show the page or text the link to the customer while you’re still with them.
  3. Ask them to tap a star rating and follow the instructions – it takes ~10 seconds.
  4. 4–5★ customers are redirected to Google to leave a public review.
  5. 1–3★ customers send private feedback to your email so you can fix issues first.
Tip: Add the ReviewResQ link to your invoice template, WhatsApp messages or SMS follow-ups, and put a QR code at your front desk or in your service vehicles.
