How ReviewResQ works

We set everything up for you. Your team only needs to send customers to your link or QR code – ReviewResQ handles the rest.

Step 1
We build your custom portal
You send us your logo, business name, and Google review link. We create a branded ReviewResQ page that matches your business and routes customers based on their rating.
Step 2
Your staff shares a simple link
After each job or visit, your staff shows the page or sends a quick text. Customers tap a star rating on their own phone – it takes about 10 seconds.
Step 3
Happy → Google, unhappy → private
4–5★ ratings are redirected straight to Google to leave a public review. 1–3★ ratings are sent to a private form that emails you the feedback so you can fix issues before they hit your public rating.
