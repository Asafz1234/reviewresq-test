Common questions

If you work with customers in person and care about your Google rating, ReviewResQ is probably a fit. Here are the questions we hear most often.

Do you guarantee 5-star reviews?
No. We don’t create fake reviews or pressure anyone. We make it extremely easy for happy customers to leave a real review on Google, and give unhappy customers a private way to talk to you instead of posting publicly.
Is this allowed by Google’s policies?
ReviewResQ doesn’t edit or post reviews for customers. It simply routes people to your Google profile or to a private form. Customers always choose their own rating and write their own review.
How long does setup take?
Once you approve your demo and send us your details, most portals go live within about 24 hours. We handle the tech so you can focus on serving customers.
Do I need to install an app or log in every day?
No app, no complicated dashboards. Your team just uses a link or QR code. If you’d like a metrics dashboard later, we can enable it for you.
Can I cancel if it’s not for me?
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. If ReviewResQ isn’t helping you, send us a text and we’ll cancel for the next billing cycle.
What do you need from me to start?
Just your business name, logo (if you have one), Google review link and a contact email. We’ll build your ReviewResQ funnel and send you a demo to approve before anything goes live.
